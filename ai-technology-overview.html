Revolutionary privacy-by-design architecture maintains complete data isolation between partners. Each partner's data exists in separate encrypted databases, with relationship insights only generated through cryptographic consent protocols.

Simple Explanation:

Your personal data stays completely private from your partner unless you both agree to share it. It's like having separate bank accounts that only combine when both people put in their keys at the same time.
