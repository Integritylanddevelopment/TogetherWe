Context Injection Engine: The Personalization Core

The Context Injection Engine transforms generic AI into your personal therapeutic companion by crafting highly specific, contextually rich prompts based on your unique relationship data.
1 Data Collection & Analysis

Gathers your responses from the 20-day onboarding, daily questions, relationship patterns, and historical therapeutic interactions.

2 Pattern Recognition

Each of the 20 engines identifies relevant patterns in their domain (attachment style, communication patterns, conflict styles, etc.).

3 Context Assembly

Combines insights from all engines into a comprehensive context profile that includes your attachment style, communication patterns, triggers, strengths, and current challenges.

4 Prompt Crafting

Creates highly specific prompts that tell the AI exactly who you are, your relationship dynamics, and what kind of guidance would be most effective.

Instead of asking a generic AI: "How do I improve communication?"

The Context Injection Engine creates a personalized prompt like:

"You are counseling Sarah (anxious attachment, tends to pursue) and Mike (avoidant attachment, withdraws during conflict). Sarah's pattern: Becomes overwhelmed, seeks reassurance, fears abandonment when Mike goes quiet. Mike's pattern: Shuts down when emotions get intense, needs space to process, fears being controlled. Recent conflict: Financial decisions - Sarah wants joint planning, Mike feels pressured and avoids the conversation. Therapeutic approach needed: EFT-based, focus on underlying attachment needs, help them understand each other's protective strategies. Question: How can they discuss finances without triggering their attachment wounds?"

Complete Processing Flow

1 User Input Received

Daily questions, voice responses, relationship check-ins, or crisis situations are captured.

2 Orchestrator Activation

Pattern Analysis Orchestrator receives the input and activates relevant engines based on content analysis.

3 Parallel Engine Analysis

All 20 engines simultaneously analyze the input through their specialized lenses, each contributing domain-specific insights.

4 Memory Integration

CommandCore Memory System provides historical context, previous patterns, and therapeutic progress to enrich the analysis.

5 Context Injection

Context Injection Engine crafts personalized prompts with your specific patterns, relationship dynamics, and therapeutic needs.

6 AI Query & Response

The personalized context is sent to Claude/GPT-4, which generates highly specific, clinically informed therapeutic guidance tailored to your unique situation.

7 Personalized Delivery

Final therapeutic insights are delivered in your preferred communication style, with his/hers perspectives and actionable recommendations.

The Power of Personalization

Generic AI vs. Context-Injected AI: The difference between receiving general relationship advice and getting therapeutic guidance that feels like it was written specifically for your unique relationship.
❌ Generic AI Response

"Communication is important in relationships. Try active listening, use 'I' statements, and schedule regular check-ins to discuss issues."

✅ Context-Injected Response

"Sarah, when you feel Mike pulling away, your anxious attachment is triggered. Instead of pursuing, try: 'Mike, I notice you seem quiet. I'm feeling a bit disconnected and would love to understand what you're processing. No pressure, just whenever you're ready.' Mike, when you need space, reassure Sarah first: 'I need some time to think, but I'm not pulling away from us. Can we reconnect in an hour?'"
