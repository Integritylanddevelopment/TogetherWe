🚀 Advanced Multi-Database Architecture

🐘
PostgreSQL Core Database
What it is: Enterprise-grade relational database that serves as our primary data store with ACID compliance (Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, Durability) ensuring data integrity.
Why it matters: Handles complex relationship data with JSON support for flexible schemas, logical partitioning for privacy, and proven scalability to billions of records. Critical for maintaining data consistency across millions of couples' sensitive information.
🔴
Redis Performance Engine
What it is: In-memory data structure store providing sub-millisecond response times for real-time operations like session management and live partner synchronization.
Why it matters: Enables instant AI responses, real-time messaging between partners, and seamless user experience. Without Redis, our platform would feel sluggish compared to social media apps users expect.
🔥
Firebase Real-Time Sync
What it is: Google's real-time database that automatically synchronizes data between partners' devices instantly, even with poor internet connections.
Why it matters: When one partner completes a therapy exercise, the other sees updates immediately. Offline support ensures couples can access content anywhere, critical for relationship continuity during travel or connectivity issues.
🧮
Vector AI Database
What it is: Specialized database storing high-dimensional emotional embeddings - mathematical representations of feelings, communication patterns, and relationship dynamics.
Why it matters: Enables AI to find similar couples, predict relationship outcomes, and provide personalized therapy recommendations. Like Netflix recommendations but for relationship health - this is our competitive secret sauce.
🔒 Triple-Database Privacy Architecture

Our revolutionary privacy-first design keeps individual data separate while enabling powerful couple insights

👤
Partner A Database
Individual emotional patterns, personal therapy history, private reflections, crisis indicators - completely isolated and encrypted
💑
Couples/Membership Database
Shared insights, joint goals, compatibility scores, mutual consent settings - only data both partners agree to share
👤
Partner B Database
Individual emotional patterns, personal therapy history, private reflections, crisis indicators - completely isolated and encrypted
Security Benefits:
  • Individual Privacy: Your partner cannot access your private thoughts or therapy sessions
  • Mutual Consent: Shared data requires explicit agreement from both partners
  • Data Portability: Each partner can export only their own data if relationship ends
  • Breach Protection: Even if one database is compromised, individual privacy remains intact
