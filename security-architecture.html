Why Triple Database?

Current Problem

Traditional couples therapy apps share everything between partners, creating:

  • Privacy concerns
  • Hesitation to be honest
  • Power imbalances
  • Trust issues with data

Our Solution

Three separate databases ensure:

  • Individual privacy protection
  • Safe spaces for honest reflection
  • Controlled sharing mechanisms
  • Dual permission protocols

Privacy + Connection = Better Therapy Outcomes

Triple Database Architecture

Partner 1 Database

Private thoughts, individual assessments, personal therapy progress

  • Personal reflections
  • Individual triggers
  • Private goals
  • Therapy history

Couple Database

AI therapist analyzes both partners' data to provide relationship insights, with dual permission required to access results

  • AI-generated therapy content
  • Relationship analysis
  • Crisis prevention protocols
  • Joint progress insights

Partner 2 Database

Separate private space for individual growth and reflection

  • Personal insights
  • Individual patterns
  • Private concerns
  • Personal development

AI Therapist Processing

How the Digital Therapist Works

Step 1: AI Analysis

Digital therapist analyzes both partners' private data to understand relationship dynamics

Step 2: Generate Insights

AI creates relationship insights and crisis prevention protocols in couple database

Step 3: Dual Permission Access

Both partners must consent before accessing AI-generated therapy content and insights

Step 4: Private Data Protected

Individual databases remain completely private - only AI insights are shared with consent

AI reads all. Partners control access to insights.
